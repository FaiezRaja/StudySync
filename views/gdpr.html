GDPR Privacy Policy

What data is collected?

StudySync collects:

  • Personal identifying information (name & email address)

How is your data collected?

StudySync collects all the data directly from you. This is done when you:

  • Create an account
  • View this website via your browser's cookies

How will your data be used?

StudySync collects your data to:

  • Add an name to your account which is used when joining a room

How will your data be stored?

Your data will be stored in a secure MySQL database

What are your Data Protection Rights?

You can find them here

How do we use cookies?

Information from you is collected through cookies (or similar technology) automatically when you use this website. This is done to:

  • Keep you signed in
  • Remember your name when you join a room

What types of cookies are used?

StudySync uses:

  • Strictly necessary cookies - cookies that are necessary for browsing the website and using its features, such as accessing secured areas of the site.
  • Functionality cookies - cookies which allow StudySync to remember the choices you have made previously (website theme such as light or dark mode)

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, but this website will not function without them

Changes to our privacy policy

Our privacy policy is kept under regular review and any updates will be placed on this webpage. This privacy policy was last updated on 19th April 2023.

How to Contact Us:

If you have any queries regarding StudySync's privacy policy or the data we may have collected from you, or if you wish to exercise your data protection rights, please feel free to contact us at MFR942@student.bham.ac.uk.

How to reach out to the relevant authority

If you have a complaint or if you feel that GeoQuiz has not resolved your concern in a satisfactory way, you can contact the Information Commissioner's office by visiting this website: Information Commissioner's Office.
